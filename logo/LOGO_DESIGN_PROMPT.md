```

Design requirements:
- A stylized fennec fox head/face as the main icon
- Minimalist, clean design with smooth curves

- Modern tech aesthetic similar to FastAPI's style
- The fox ears should be prominent (fennec foxes have large ears)
- Suitable for both light and dark backgrounds
- Vector-style, flat design
- Professional and trustworthy appearance

Text requirements:
- Include the word "FENNEC" in a modern sans-serif font
- Text should be clean and readable
- Can be placed next to or below the icon
- Optional tagline: "Small, Swift, Adaptable"

Style inspiration:
- Similar to FastAPI's clean, modern logo
- Tech-forward and professional
- Suitable for documentation, GitHub, and PyPI
- Should work well at different sizes (favicon to banner)

Color palette:
- Primary: Purple to pink gradient (#667eea → #764ba2)
- Accent: White or light colors for contrast
- Background: Transparent or white

The logo should convey:
- Speed and agility (like a fennec fox)
- Modern Python development
- Reliability and professionalism
- Tunisian origin (subtle, not obvious)
```

### Detailed Specifications

#### Icon Design:
- **Subject**: Fennec fox head (front-facing or 3/4 view)
- **Key Features**:
  - Large, prominent ears (fennec's signature feature)
  - Friendly, intelligent expression
  - Simplified geometric shapes
  - Smooth, flowing lines
  
#### Color Specifications:
- **Primary Gradient**: 
  - Start: #667eea (Purple)
  - End: #764ba2 (Pink/Magenta)
- **Gradient Direction**: Diagonal (135 degrees) or top-to-bottom
- **Accent Colors**: White (#FFFFFF) for highlights
- **Background**: Transparent or white

#### Typography:
- **Font Style**: Modern sans-serif (similar to Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat)
- **Font Weight**: 600-700 (Semi-bold to Bold)
- **Text**: "FENNEC" or "Fennec"
- **Optional Tagline**: "Small, Swift, Adaptable" (lighter weight)

#### Layout Options:

**Option 1 - Horizontal:**
```
[Fox Icon] FENNEC
```

**Option 2 - Stacked:**
```
  [Fox Icon]
   FENNEC
```

**Option 3 - Badge Style:**
```
┌─────────────┐
│  [Fox Icon] │
│   FENNEC    │
└─────────────┘
```

#### Size Variations Needed:
- **Banner**: 1200x400px (for GitHub, documentation)
- **Square**: 512x512px (for PyPI, social media)
- **Favicon**: 64x64px (for website)
- **Icon Only**: 256x256px (for app icons)

#### Style References:
- **FastAPI**: Clean, modern, professional
- **Django**: Solid, trustworthy
- **Flask**: Simple, elegant
- **Vue.js**: Friendly, approachable

#### What to Avoid:
- ❌ Overly complex details
- ❌ Too many colors
- ❌ Cartoon-style or childish look
- ❌ Hard-to-read fonts
- ❌ Cluttered design
- ❌ Low contrast

#### What to Include:
- ✅ Clean, professional appearance
- ✅ Memorable and distinctive
- ✅ Scalable to different sizes
- ✅ Works in monochrome
- ✅ Represents speed and agility
- ✅ Modern tech aesthetic
